Quartiles and quartiles range
Are the 3 point that split the data set into 4 equal groups
Q1 = middle number between the smallest number and its median
Q2 = is the median
Q3 = middle number between the median and its largest number

1-find the median (if is odd, do not include the median in either half)
2-Fin the median of the first quarter and second quarter
3-Define quartiles
4-find Inter Quartile range (IQR = Q3 - Q1)
